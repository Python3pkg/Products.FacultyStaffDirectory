For ZopeSkel 2.11.1 and earlier:

You should examine your setup.py for the following code:

      entry_points="""
      # -*- entry_points -*- 
      [distutils.setup_keywords]
      paster_plugins = setuptools.dist:assert_string_list

      [egg_info.writers]
      paster_plugins.txt = setuptools.command.egg_info:write_arg
      """,

You should remove the distutils.setup_keywords and egg_info.writers entry points to avoid buildout-related problems when you use your product:

      entry_points="""
      # -*- entry_points -*- 
      """,

To avoid having to include your product's ZCML manually or via a zcml= slug in buildout, you should add an entry_point for the z3c.autoinclude.plugin (note, this is new for Plone 3.3 and greater):

      entry_points="""
      # -*- entry_points -*- 
      [z3c.autoinclude.plugin]
      target = plone
      """,
